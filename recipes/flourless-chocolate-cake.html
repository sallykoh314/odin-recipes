Flourless Chocolate Cake

Description

This flourless chocolate cake recipe is very easy to make and only requires 4 ingredients. It doesn't require any type of flour and is gluten free. This is one of my favorite chocolate cake because it is very airy and light but also fills your mouth with decadent dark chocolate.

This recipe yields one 8in cake pan and serves about 6-8 people. It takes about 15 minutes to make the batter and 30 minutes to bake. This is the perfect elegant chocolate cake to make when you're hosting a party.

Ingredients

  • 188g Dark Chocolate
  • 94g Butter
  • 3g Salt

  • 85g Egg yolks
  • 53g Sugar

  • 180g Egg whites
  • 53g Sugar

Method of Production

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Spray an 8" cake pan with nonstick oil and line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper.
  3. Combine chocolate, butter, and salt into a heat-safe bowl and melt over a double boiler.
  4. Create a meringue by whipping the egg whites and 53g of sugar with a whisk or in a stand mixer on medium high speed to a medium peak.
  5. Whip egg yolks and 53g of sugar to ribbon stage or until it is pale white and airy.
  6. Fold the whipped yolks into the meringue until incorporated.
  7. Fold in the melted chocolate and butter into the egg and meringue mixture until everything is incorporated.
  8. Pour the batter into the lined cake pan and place it in the middle rack of the oven and bake for about 30 minutes or until the center springs back when poked.
  9. Remove cake from the oven and let it cool completely before taking it out of the pan.
  10. After the cake is removed from the pan, dust it with a generous amount of powdered sugar and sliced into 6-8 pieces and enjoy!
